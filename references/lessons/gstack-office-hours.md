demand reality, status quo, desperate specificity, narrowest wedge, observation,
and future-fit. Builder mode: design thinking brainstorming for side projects,

## What Will Claude Ask You?

Claude will walk you through a structured conversation. Key questions include:

- *"gstack browse needs a one-time build (~10 seconds). OK to proceed?"*
- *"You said 'enterprises in healthcare.' Can you name one specific person at one specific company?"*
- *"what's the smallest demo that gets your VP/sponsor to greenlight the project?"*
- *"does this survive a reorg — or does it die when your champion leaves?"*
- *"What's the strongest evidence you have that someone actually wants this — not 'is interested,' not 'signed up for a waitlist,' but would be genuinely upset if it disappeared tomorrow?"*
- *"Should we build on this prior design or start fresh?"*
